# Career Archetypes

## BUILDER
**Core Identity:** Hands-on creators who bring ideas to life through tangible products and systems.

**Key Traits:**
- Thrives on seeing concrete results of their work
- Enjoys working with tools, technologies, and frameworks
- Problem-solver who prefers practical solutions over theoretical discussions
- Gets satisfaction from shipping products and features

**Motivations:**
- Creating things that work and serve a purpose
- Continuous improvement and iteration
- Mastering technical skills and craftsmanship
- Autonomy in execution

**Strengths:**
- Technical implementation
- Product development
- System architecture
- Debugging and optimization

**Typical Careers:** Software Engineer, Product Developer, Engineer, Maker, Craftsperson, Technical Architect

---

## CONNECTOR
**Core Identity:** Natural networkers who create value by bringing people, ideas, and opportunities together.

**Key Traits:**
- Energized by meeting new people and building relationships
- Sees opportunities in connecting disparate dots
- Strong emotional intelligence and communication skills
- Thrives in collaborative, social environments

**Motivations:**
- Building meaningful professional relationships
- Facilitating mutually beneficial introductions
- Being at the center of communities and networks
- Creating opportunities for others

**Strengths:**
- Relationship building
- Communication and persuasion
- Community building
- Partnership development

**Typical Careers:** Business Development, Sales, Recruiter, Community Manager, Partnership Manager, Networker, Event Organizer

---

## ANALYST
**Core Identity:** Data-driven thinkers who uncover insights and make decisions based on rigorous analysis.

**Key Traits:**
- Loves diving deep into data and finding patterns
- Methodical and detail-oriented approach
- Prefers evidence-based decision making
- Enjoys solving complex puzzles through logic

**Motivations:**
- Discovering hidden insights in data
- Providing clarity through quantitative analysis
- Optimizing systems and processes
- Being the expert who provides answers

**Strengths:**
- Data analysis and interpretation
- Critical thinking and logic
- Research and investigation
- Pattern recognition

**Typical Careers:** Data Analyst, Business Analyst, Researcher, Scientist, Financial Analyst, Strategy Consultant, Market Researcher

---

## CREATOR
**Core Identity:** Imaginative innovators who express ideas through original content and creative work.

**Key Traits:**
- Driven by self-expression and originality
- Thinks outside the box and challenges conventions
- Values aesthetic quality and impact
- Needs creative freedom and flexibility

**Motivations:**
- Producing original, meaningful work
- Expressing unique perspectives and ideas
- Evoking emotions and inspiring others
- Pushing creative boundaries

**Strengths:**
- Content creation
- Creative problem-solving
- Storytelling and communication
- Innovation and ideation

**Typical Careers:** Designer, Writer, Artist, Content Creator, Marketing Creative, UX Designer, Brand Strategist, Filmmaker

---

## LEADER
**Core Identity:** Strategic guides who inspire teams and drive organizational success through vision and direction.

**Key Traits:**
- Takes ownership and responsibility naturally
- Comfortable making high-stakes decisions
- Inspires and motivates others toward goals
- Thinks strategically about long-term direction

**Motivations:**
- Driving impact at scale
- Developing and empowering team members
- Setting vision and achieving ambitious goals
- Building high-performing teams

**Strengths:**
- Strategic thinking
- Decision-making under pressure
- People management and development
- Vision setting and execution

**Typical Careers:** Manager, Director, Executive, Team Lead, Founder, General Manager, Department Head

---

## SUPPORTER
**Core Identity:** Empathetic helpers who find fulfillment in enabling others' success and well-being.

**Key Traits:**
- Genuinely enjoys helping others succeed
- High empathy and active listening skills
- Patient and service-oriented mindset
- Finds meaning in making others' lives easier

**Motivations:**
- Making a positive difference in people's lives
- Solving problems that help others
- Building trust and being reliable
- Creating supportive environments

**Strengths:**
- Customer service excellence
- Empathy and emotional support
- Problem resolution
- Training and mentoring

**Typical Careers:** Customer Success, HR Professional, Teacher, Counselor, Support Specialist, Customer Service, Healthcare Worker, Social Worker

---

## VISIONARY
**Core Identity:** Big-picture thinkers who imagine future possibilities and challenge the status quo.

**Key Traits:**
- Constantly thinking about what could be
- Comfortable with ambiguity and uncertainty
- Sees connections between seemingly unrelated concepts
- Driven by innovation and transformation

**Motivations:**
- Pioneering new approaches and ideas
- Solving problems no one else is addressing
- Creating paradigm shifts
- Inspiring change at a fundamental level

**Strengths:**
- Strategic foresight
- Innovation and ideation
- Conceptual thinking
- Inspiring and evangelizing ideas

**Typical Careers:** Entrepreneur, Strategic Planner, Innovation Lead, Futurist, Chief Strategy Officer, Startup Founder, Product Visionary

---

## ORGANIZER
**Core Identity:** Systematic coordinators who bring order to chaos through structure and process.

**Key Traits:**
- Loves creating systems and processes
- Detail-oriented with strong planning skills
- Thrives on efficiency and optimization
- Finds satisfaction in smooth operations

**Motivations:**
- Creating order and predictability
- Maximizing efficiency and productivity
- Ensuring nothing falls through the cracks
- Building reliable, repeatable systems

**Strengths:**
- Project management
- Process optimization
- Planning and coordination
- Attention to detail

**Typical Careers:** Project Manager, Operations Manager, Program Coordinator, Event Planner, Chief of Staff, Logistics Manager, Process Improvement Specialist

---

## Notes

- Most people are a blend of 2-3 archetypes with one primary archetype
- Archetypes can evolve throughout a career as interests and priorities shift
- Understanding your archetype helps in career planning, role selection, and work style optimization
- No archetype is better than another - each brings unique value to organizations and teams
